The Best Movie All of Time

My Top 3 Movies according to Wahid Nurhisyam

Fish Story

A Breathtaking Tale of the Deep

I've watched this movie more than three times, and I'm still hooked! The story is so engaging and unpredictable that you'll be on the edge of your seat until the very end.

Memento

A Mind-Bending Mystery

Christopher Nolan's genius shines through in this movie, which masterfully weaves together past and future to create a thrilling narrative that will keep you guessing until the very end.

Beyond the Infinite Two Minutes

A Timeless Festival Favorite

This movie is a wild ride from start to finish, with twists and turns that will keep you on your toes until the very end. It's a must-see for any film enthusiast!
